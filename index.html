School Clubs

Few School Clubs That Are Currently Active In School at Present.

Little Kites

The Little Kites Program nurtures IT interest in students, molding future tech leaders with workshops and projects. It signals a commitment to education and innovation, empowering minds for a tech-driven world.

Junior Red Cross

Junior Red Cross inspires young helpers, nurturing kindness through community service. Guiding children to aid others, it fosters leadership and solidarity, embodying the spirit of humanitarianism.

The Kerala Bharat Scouts and Guides

The Kerala Bharat Scouts and Guides organization empowers youth through leadership and community service, instilling values of teamwork, resilience, and compassion in individuals and communities.

Science Club

The Science Club organizes workshops, quizzes, and classes to nurture a love for science. Through engaging activities, students explore the fascinating realms of chemistry, physics, and biology.
